The Setting

Circuit

A series of cities among which prostituted people are moved. For example, the West Coast circuit which include San Diego, Las Vegas, Portland, and the cities between. The term can also mean a chain of states. The Minisota pipeline moves victims from state to state until they arrive in New York.

Escort Service

An organization that operates via phone. Victims are sent out to the buyer or the buyer comes by to pick her up. Some Services partner with others to have enough women for large parties or events.

Kiddie Stroll

An area that is know for prostituting young girls.

Track

An area know for it's prostitution activity.

The Victim

Bottom

The pimp's "right hand." She is appointed by the pimp to supervise over the other women. She helps instruct victims, collect money, book hotel rooms, post ads, or punish the other girls.

Branding

A tatoo or carving on the victim that indicates ownership to the pimp.

Renegade

Prostitute without a pimp.

Squaring up

Attempt to escape the game.

Trick

Turn Out

The Pimp

Automatic

When a victim's pimp is out of town or in jail the victim is expected to act by the rules. Most victims do act "automatic" for fear of punishment or have been psychologically manipulated or feel a sense of loyalty to the pimp.

Caught A Case

When a pimp or victim have been arrested and charged.

Choosing Up

Accrding to traditional pimping rules if a victim makes eye contact with another pimp than she is choosing him to be her new pimp. When the new pimp takes ownership of the victim he is "choosing up." If the original pimp wants the victim back he needs to pay the new pimp a fee.

Daddy

What a victim calls her pimp.

Finesse Pimp/Romeo Pimp

A pimp which controlls his victims through psychological manipulation.

Gorilla (or Guerilla) Pimp

Pimp which controlls his victims through physical violence.

Pimp Circle

When a group of pimps encircle a victim to intimidate her.

Seasoning

Designed to break down a victim to ensure compliance through psychological manipulation, intimidation, gang rape, sodomy, beatings, deprivation of food or sleep and/or isolation from friends or family.

Trade Up/Trade Down

To trade a victim between pimps. She may be traded for another girl or for money.

Brothel

Refers to an facility where sex is sold. Brothels have security to keep women and children in and robbers out. Brothels can be open up to 24 hours a day. If closed than the victims are locked from the outside in.

Family/Folks

What a group under the control of the same pimp call themselves.

Stable

A group of victims under the control of the same pimp.

Wifeys

What a women and/or girls call each other under the control of the same pimp.

Madam

A woman who manages a brothel, escrot service, or any other type of human trafficking.

Exit Fee

To discourge their victims from leaving pimps demand money from a victim who is tring to leave the game. Most victims never leave freely.

Out of Pocket

A known prostitute who is workin on a pimps track with out belonging to the pimp. She is intimidated until she chooses a pimp. This also refers to a victim who is not following the rules.

Quota

A set amount of money a victim has to make each night before coming home. She is expected to hand over all the money she has made to her pimp. If she fails to meet the quota she is sent back out until she meets it. Quotas are set by geographical location and event.

Date

When the act of prostitution is taking place the victim is said to be "on a date."
